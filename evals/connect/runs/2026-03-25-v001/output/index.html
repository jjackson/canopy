How It Works

Learn. Deliver. Verify & Pay.

Three steps. One platform. Complete accountability from training through compensation.

1

Learn

Workers select and receive training through the integrated digital platform. Skills development on their schedule, at their pace.

2

Deliver

Workers deliver health and community services using CommCare for data collection and real-time workflow management in the field.

3

Verify & Pay

Service delivery is verified through biometrics and GPS. Workers receive fair, transparent compensation — managed end-to-end on the platform.
