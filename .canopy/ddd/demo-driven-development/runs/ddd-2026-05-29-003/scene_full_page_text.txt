AI writes code fast but drifts from intent, and a human cannot meaningfully review a wall of code or markdown. DDD makes one cohesive, multi-persona narrative the single artifact that is simultaneously the design doc, the video script, and the build plan; gets the human to agree to that story on an editable web surface BEFORE anything is built; then renders the demo, has independent judges score it, and self-improves until it is good enough to ship as an explainer video. The hard parts are keeping the narrative honest (grounded vs aspirational) and making human review fast and high-leverage.
